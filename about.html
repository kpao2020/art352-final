Ken Pao

With over 10 years of badminton practice, I am happy to share my badminton journey and hope to bring this healthy sport to the border community.

Health is especially important, and I try to keep myself healthy with my favorite sport - badminton. I might not be as competitive as others, but it is all about making new friends and enriching my badminton journey. Sometimes when I am not playing, I will go to the gym to keep myself active.

I tend to love a lot of unhealthy food, such as ice cream, boba drink, hot pots, wings, and more, but in recent years, I have changed my diet to be a more balancing plan and hope to have a healthier life.

Besides badminton, I am an IT engineer in my professional life, a US Navy veteran, and a Nintendo switch gamer. I love my work as I make new friends every week and learn new things along the way. As a Navy veteran, I traveled to over 100 cities in 40 countries and bringing my diverse experience to assist the civilian world wherever I see fit. In my leisure time, I enjoy playing Nintendo switch games with my family and friends.

Being a new graduate student in CSUEB, I hope to make new friends and enjoy playing badminton together.
