My Badminton Journey

Begin playing

Whether you are playing for the first time or just for fun, badminton is easy to learn and has many benefits. This is how I got started my badminton journey.

  • Healthy exercise
  • Release stress
  • Strengthen muscles

Advanced Training

Challenge yourself to the best! The more I play, the more I love badminton. I started looking for advanced training and participating in tournaments and events.

  • Increase social circle
  • Improves reflexes
  • Stay active

Badminton is for everyone

Anyone can play and enjoy badminton. You can play in a single, double, or in a group setting.

As I learn and develop more badminton technique, I started to share knowledge and coach in high school. Spreading the joy of playing badminton and giving back to the community.

Even I move on to my college and professional life, I continue to dedicate time for badminton and share the badminton happiness wherever possible.
